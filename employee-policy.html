Code of Conduct

The HRFlow Code of Conduct serves as our guide for professional behavior and ethics. We expect every team member to embody these values in their daily work and interactions.

1. Professional Ethics

We believe in maintaining the highest standards of integrity. This includes:

  • Honesty and transparency in all business dealings.
  • Respect for intellectual property and confidentiality.
  • Avoiding personal interests that conflict with company goals.

2. Inclusive Culture

HRFlow is a sanctuary for diversity. We strictly prohibit discrimination, bullying, or harassment. Our workplace is built on mutual respect and psychological safety for all employees.

3. Resource Stewardship

Employees are responsible for the careful use of company assets, including hardware, software, and intellectual resources. Misuse of company property for personal gain is a violation of this code.
