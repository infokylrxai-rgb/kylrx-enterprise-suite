IT Asset & Data Privacy Policy

Effective Date: Oct 01, 2026 • Version 1.2

PENDING ACTION

This policy outlines the guidelines and requirements for the proper use and security of IT assets and data within HRFlow. All employees are required to adhere to these standards to protect organizational integrity and data privacy.

1. Hardware Usage

Employees are provided with laptops and peripherals for business purposes only. Maintenance and physical security of these assets are the responsibility of the assigned employee.

2. Data Security & Encryption

All organizational data must be stored on approved cloud platforms. Local storage of sensitive information is strictly prohibited without explicit AES-256 encryption approval.

3. Software Installation

Installation of unauthorized third-party software is prohibited. All software must be vetted by the IT Compliance team.

4. Digital Privacy

HRFlow reserves the right to monitor organizational assets for compliance and security purposes. Personal use of assets should be kept to a minimum.
