📧 Email Configuration

Configure your organization's email domain so all system emails are sent from your own address (e.g. hr@yourcompany.com) instead of a generic Gmail.

Why configure this?
Without this, onboarding emails go to Spam because they're sent from a shared Gmail. Set your organization's SMTP or Resend API here so all emails — onboarding, payroll, leave approvals — land directly in the inbox with your company's email domain.
This is what employees will see as the sender.
